# Updating the Modpack

### Modrinth App

The linked modpack will display an "Update" button

### Prism Launcher

- Select the instance
- Click on "Edit"
- Go to the "Modrinth" tab
- Update to version
- Press "Update Pack"

### Minecraft

Backup your worlds, screenshots, and world-map data. Then go through the reinstallation process: https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/corecraft/latest/docs/installation#minecraft-launcher
