# Troubleshooting

Before reporting to us, please report to the respective authors as they are in control of their project and it is better for the bugs to be fixed in their project than just our modpack. If you are sure it is an occurrence with our modpack, please review the following: 

- Crash: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Crash
- Troubleshooting: https://ftb.fandom.com/wiki/Troubleshooting

If you have logs, please upload them to https://mclo.gs/ then share the link. mclo.gs automatically removes sensitive information such as IP addresses.

Specific or personal inquiries can be answered in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/Gwr9AdFEzz).
